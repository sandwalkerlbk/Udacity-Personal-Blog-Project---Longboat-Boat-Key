10 Miles of Beautiful Beaches

Longboat Key’s white sand beaches beckon visitors every day of the year. During a walk near the lapping shores of the turquoise Gulf of Mexico, you’re likely to stumble across a freshly made sandcastle, a fly-fisher in search of the perfect catch, a sailboat breezily passing in the distance and any other beach-related activities you can dream up. The gorgeous white sand beaches of Longboat Key are enjoyed by residents and guests at LbK’s fantastic resorts, hotels, condos and beach houses.

Longboat Key's beach is lined with private homes, condominiums and rental properties that limit public access to the beach. There are a number of public beach access points on Longboat Key leading to the Gulf of Mexico beaches. There are 12 beach access points on Gulf of Mexico Dr. None of the public beach access points have any facilities such as bathrooms, concessions or lifeguards. Although, there are restrooms at Bayfront Park parking area. The island is divided with two county lines--Sarasota and Manattee. Most residents tend to think ot the beaches as those on the south side of the island or the Sarasota beaches and those on the north Manattee side otherwise known as Conrad Beach.

South End Beach

The south end of the island offers beautiful white quartz wide beaches. The first couple miles of the beach area are private access only and dedicated to guests and members of the famous Longbaot Key Beach Club; with such amenities as two restaruatens, pool and bar area and world-class golfing. This beach area is groomed daily and offers water sports such as paddleboarding and kayaking to its guests. Want to see more photos of this area? North End Beaches: Whitney & Conrad Beach

Whitney Beach, towards the north end of the key across from Whitney Plaza, has public parking, and there are a number of well-marked beach access areas. Although there are no amenities, these beaches offer beautiful white sands, graceful sea oats, sparkling Gulf waters and refreshing breezes. At the farthest northern tip is an area locals refer to Conrad Beach, an area that offers a truely undisturbed beach experience. Click below to see more photos of this pristine area.
