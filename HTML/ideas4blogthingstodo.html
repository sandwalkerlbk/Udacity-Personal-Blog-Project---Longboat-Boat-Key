Dining

Longboat Key offers tons of dining options - on the island itself there are about about 10 yummy resaurants. Dining options are also found nearby the island on both the south end and the north - Anna Maria Islands offers almost unlimited dining options and St. Armand circle is a world reknown shopiing and dining experieince only minutes away in Sarasots county.

Shopping

St Armand Circles offers a full shopping expereience for all - from expesive jewlry to dicount beachwear and shell souvenirs. Enjoy some a cool marquirta while you shop or an ice crem code for the kiddos.

And More!

Longboat Key is minutes away for the Mote Aquarium a favorite for the whole family. Also nearby in Sarasota is the Caad Zaan Museam and Ringling Bros museam of art.
